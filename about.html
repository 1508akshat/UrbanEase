About UrbanEase

Our Story

UrbanEase was born out of a simple insight: city living should feel inspiring, not overwhelming. We loved the curation of Zomato and the premium feel of CRED, so we combined the best of both worlds into one dark, modern experience that celebrates every kind of urban explorer.
